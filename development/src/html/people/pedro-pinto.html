Pedro Pinto

Project Architect

Architecture is an act of making culture, of shared humanity. To provide and share shelter from the outside world, to provide and share shelter for the Inside World.

At several points along the journey we’ve asked ourselves again those apparently ‘simple’ questions . Our answers continue to come back to a few principles and aspirations that are at the heart of what we do. Each month we meet as a team and we review our progress under the headings of Art, Human, Learnings and Enough.
