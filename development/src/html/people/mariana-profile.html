Mariana Matos

Architect

Mariana joined Cooney Architects in 2018 and is a registered Architect. Mariana graduated in 2015 with a Masters in Architecture, at FAUP (Faculty of Architecture, University of Porto).

Previously, Mariana worked in Portugal and England, mainly in residential and conservation projects. Since she joined Cooney Architects, she has been working in sports, cultural and housing projects.
